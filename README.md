# Buty Car Wash

To thank the FiveM community for its continued support, we are releasing this advanced and completely free Car Wash system.

Buty Car Wash features an immersive vehicle cleaning experience with animated NPC workers, cinematic camera sequences, particle effects, and a modern user interface. Choose from multiple service packages and watch as your vehicle is professionally cleaned with unique animations and interactions.

## Features

* Advanced and immersive car wash system
* Modern and customizable NUI interface
* Multiple wash packages (Basic, Standard, Premium)
* Animated NPC workers
* Particle effects and props
* Cinematic camera sequences
* Configurable locations, prices, and NPCs
* ESX support
* QBCore support
* QBox support
* Optimized for performance
* Completely free

## Configuration

The resource is highly configurable through the included configuration file:

* Framework selection
* Wash prices
* Car wash locations
* NPC models
* Cleaning routes and animations

## Framework Support

Supported frameworks:

* ESX
* QBCore
* QBox
